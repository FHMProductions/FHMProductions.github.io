Meet the Team

Bex Wallace

Director

Bex is a North East based development, story and casting producer. With a decade's worth of telly under her belt, Bex is an asset to any production. With a passion for story telling, Bex is great at discovering brilliant characters, gaining access to tricky places and driving narratives. She's worked on mostly primetime (first series) for the major UK Broadcasters. Being responsible for helping to shape the format of the show from casting to compliance. Bex has worked on shows like Extraordinary Portraits with Bill Bailey (BBC) Freddie Flintoff's Field of Dreams(BBC), Five Guys a Week (C4), Inside the Factory (BBC), Sixteen (C4) Charlotte in Sunderland (BBC) and Rise and Fall (C4)

Cheryl Hanson

Director

Cheryl is a Bradford based casting and talent producer. She has worked on several high-profile shows - X-Factor, BGT, 24 hours in A&E, and First Dates. Cheryl is a friendly, dedicated professional whose enthusiasm energises contestants. She isn't afraid to go that extra mile, thus ensuring she always delivers a happy and outstanding cast!

Frankie Mensah

Director

Frankie is an Essex based welfare and casting producer. With over 20 years' experience, Frankie has worked on some of the biggest shows in the industry including X-Factor, BGT, and First Dates. He understands intuitively who and what makes a great watch. He was also part of the 2022 "Breakthrough Leaders" cohort.
